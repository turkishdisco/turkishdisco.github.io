Summary

Hi! I’m Chris Dixon, currently a Research Fellow in the School of Psychology at the University of Leeds where I’m working on the DART project.

I’m a quantitative researcher with interests in the assessment, development, and intervention of children’s language and literacy skills, particularly in culturally and linguistically diverse school pupils. I’m an avid user of R and particularly the Tidyverse ecosystem of packages. I have co-run two R workshops, and facilitated research methods training at both UG and PG levels.

I grew up in South Africa, returning to my parents’ native England aged 11. I think this is what sparked my interest in languages, though I only managed to acquire one of the 11 official ones spoken there!
